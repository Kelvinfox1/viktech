About Tandao

Bridging the Digital Divide for Underserved Communities

Tandao Innovations is an ICT-based social enterprise committed to solving one of Africa’s biggest challenges: the lack of equitable access to the internet and modern technologies. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by delivering community-driven, affordable, and innovative technology infrastructure in low-income and underserved regions.

Connectivity

Deploying reliable, low-cost community internet infrastructure.

Digital Literacy

Training programs for youth, women, and marginalized groups.

Innovation

Setting up innovation labs in underserved areas.

Our Vision and Mission

Shaping an Inclusive Digital Future

Vision: To be a leading catalyst in Africa’s digital transformation, ensuring inclusive access to the internet and emerging technologies for all.

Mission: To bridge the digital divide by delivering community-driven, affordable, and innovative technology infrastructure in low-income and underserved regions.

Core Values

Inclusivity, Affordability, Sustainability, Innovation, Partnership.
